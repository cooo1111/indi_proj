What I Do

Supporting Friends

I make a conscious effort to support my friends when they are feeling down. Whether through listening, encouragement, or simply being present, I believe small actions can make a big difference.

Helping Elderly Customers

While working part-time at a coffee shop, I often notice elderly customers struggling with the ordering system or reading price tags. I take the initiative to approach them and guide them through the process, ensuring they feel comfortable and respected.

Reflection

Through my experiences, I have learned that meaningful impact does not always come from big achievements, but from consistent small actions. I value empathy, patience, and communication, and I continue to improve myself by balancing big-picture thinking with attention to detail.
